Artefatti (artifacts) are audiovisuals installations. They are made of some devices and the open-source code running on them. They are little musical and visual compositionts that ( thanks to their generative nature ) never repeat, while remaining identical to themself, or they are audiovisual instruments to be played. Ideally they are realized in the place of installations, in no more than 5 days. An "artefatto" isn't reproposed anywhare else than the place of the first installation.
