"cento sigilli" is an asemic writing laboratory I organized in the Cavallerizza Irreale for Here³ (2018), with the help of the Cavallerizza's collaborative drawing group.
The workshop lasted two days, 6 hours each day. All the people were free to come in and partecipate to a part of the workshop and then leave.
The first day we invented all the exercises and through iteration we elaborated three different alphabeths.
The second day we wrote more than 100 sigils which became part of one of the installations for the exhibition.
